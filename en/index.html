Welcome to Les Gîtes du Siècle

Our property consists of two spacious lodgings that can accommodate up to 8 people each, as well as an independent room with a private bathroom. Located in the heart of Ganges, our accommodations are accessible through charming suspended gardens that offer a green and peaceful setting, while being in the center of the city.

We offer rentals by the night or by the week, depending on your preferences. Address: 8 rue de l'Olivette, just a stone's throw from all amenities.

And to extend the pleasure, our family café welcomes you just three minutes on foot from the gîtes, for a pleasant moment with a coffee or a drink.
